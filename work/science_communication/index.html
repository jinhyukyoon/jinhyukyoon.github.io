My role: speaker, actor, content development

Based on the foundational business of Korea Foundation for the Advancement of Science & Creativity (KOFAC), science communication focuses on presenting science to the public with ease. I participated developing and performing contents such as talk, street performance, and play.
I mainly focused on delivering publich speech about mechanical engineering, such as robotics, bioengineering, control, machine learning, and science communication as visiting middle school, high school, library and science institution. Audience varies from elementary and middle school student to office worker, teacher, and public.
Speech material contains infographic, video, story and audience participation through game and science experiment for interaction. Audience survey shows that talk contains many unfamiliar and complex scientific knowledge but can be easily understood. List of the talk is shown below.
