Entropy

Art work at 2017 Artience Creation Challenge 30x30, 30 hours straight collaboration work with one artist. To express challenge topic, ageing, we chose entropy which measures disorder and amount of useless.

Runners up - 2017 Artience Creation Challenge 30x30

2017. 09

Entropy

My role: shooting equipment setup, performance directing, scenario writing
Collaborator: YuKung Jung
Entropy is about measuring disorder and amount of useless. In this film, we tried to show increase of entropy as a person getting aged. Performer fills paint named life on white drawing paper named time, as being covered up with rope named disorder. Just as life, performer shows her agony as holding and dropping brush or trying to break the rope. However, inside of irrefutable, natural flow of entropy, she finally accepts disorder at ease which fully covered up her body.

2017. 02 - 2017. 05

Book Borrowing Robot

My role: software develop, presentation
Collaborator: YoungSin Kim, SeHwan Rho, DooHee Kim, and DongHyeon Lim
Robot consisted of gripper, camera, 3 number of MCU, and motor. With information of bookshelf coordination, perform automatic drive to near the bookshelf. After that perform precise drive with camera vision control until specific distance. Finally drag book with gripper and rewind previous driving process.
