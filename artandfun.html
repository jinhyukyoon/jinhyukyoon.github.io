Entropy

Video art work expresses ageing through entropy

2017

Entropy

My role: shooting equipment setup, performance directing, scenario writing
Collaborator: Yukung Jung
Art work at 2017 Artience Creation Challenge 30x30, 30 hours straight collaboration work with one artist hosted by British Council. To express challenge topic ageing, we chose entropy which measures disorder and amount of useless.
Entropy is about measuring disorder and amount of useless. In this film, we tried to show increase of entropy as a person getting aged. Performer fills paint named life on white drawing paper named time, as being covered up with rope named disorder. Just as life, performer shows her agony as holding and dropping brush or trying to break the rope. However, inside of irrefutable, natural flow of entropy, she finally accepts disorder at ease which fully covered up her body.
