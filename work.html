Sovoro

Auto transcription software for people with hearing loss

2018

Sovoro

My role: UI/UX design, catalog design, market research, business activity
Collaborator: Jihyeon Yoon, Seungman Choi, and Yongtaek Kwon

Sovoro is a social startup company, developing auto transcription software and app using realtime speech-to-text voice recognition engine. Service targets people with hearing loss who want to use software in on-offline lecture and seminar. Since existing solutions, such as stenography or sign language, are expensive and inefficient for daily education, Sovoro plays meaningful role as a social service.
Book Borrowing Robot

Supports book borrowing process at the library

2017

Book Borrowing Robot

My role: software develop, presentation
Collaborator: Youngsin Kim, Sehwan Rho, Doohee Kim, and Donghyeon Lim
Robot consisted of gripper, camera, 3 number of MCU, and motor. With information of bookshelf coordination, perform automatic drive to near the bookshelf. After that perform precise drive with camera vision control until specific distance. Finally drag book with gripper and rewind previous driving process.
